Biography

A hard-working, inquisitive, professional engineer with a passion for systems improvement who strives to have far-reaching impact

For the past six years Melissa has had the opportunity to learn about cancer care and oncology safety firsthand through direct involvement in a number of projects sponsored by several Canadian cancer centres. She has been able to develop strong relationships with several oncology leaders, and has a solid understanding of end-to-end medical oncology processes, right from chemotherapy ordering through to administration.

She has worked closely with the Juravinski Hospital & Cancer Centre, CancerCare Manitoba, Cancer Care Nova Scotia, and several other cancer agencies who participated in the Chemotherapy Admixture Safety Study.

Melissa is passionate about improving cancer care, has extensive experience applying human factors methods and frameworks to real-world challenges, and thrives in fast-paced environments.
